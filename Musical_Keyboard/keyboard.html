QWERTYsynth was built by me, Ian Marshall.

I'm a high school music teacher turned stay-at-home dad who is learning web development while at home.

In my free time, I enjoy playing drums in the world-famous jazz combo Standards & Substandards.
QWERTYsynth was built to be used on a desktop computer and doesn't work well on mobile.

For best results, make sure you're using Google Chrome as your browser. If you're having problems with browser shortcuts triggering while playing, there are Chrome extensions available that can disable those shortcuts.

To report any other bugs, feel free to contact me at imarshall18@gmail.com.
